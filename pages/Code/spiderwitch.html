Spider Witch

This is a visual novel about a young witch wanting to befriend the local neighborhood kid, Jun Wu. Players can choose to play as Snow, Wilhelm, or Sage. Most of the game play is heavily dialogue based. It's made in the Renpy visual novel software. I never made a full game before this one, so this was became something I wanted to try to satisfy my curiosity. ヽ(・∀・)ﾉ
