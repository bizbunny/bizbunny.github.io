Eggy and Egglette

Idea

A lot of the characters featured in this game stem from doodles from my childhood, especially Eggy and Egglette. The overall idea I wanted to get across was of staying curious, espcially about other cultures. Because of how silly the characters inherirently are, I decided to approach the overall feel of the game like a children's storybook. But I still wanted to experiment, so I approached the art through pixel art.

At that point, the todo list was made to keep my priorities in order and make sure I get the core tasks done:

  • Decide on basic story for the game
  • Flesh out writing
  • Sketch out art assets
  • Add inventory system
  • Add basic map system
  • Render art assets
Implementation

Since I wanted the feel of a storybook, I wanted to approach this project through making a visual novel.
