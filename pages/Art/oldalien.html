Rena The Alien Queen (Old Version)

From the same 2018 class, I wanted to design an even older character I thought up as a kid when watching Martin Mystery on Teletoon. An alien queen I named Rena. The initial idea was a queen of an alien race, so the design process started with her wearing a fancy dress. But after some feedback, I fleshed out her design to make her more fish like while I made her attire a bit reminiscent of Wonderwoman (2017 movie).
