Rena The Alien Queen

From the same 2018 class, I wanted to redesign an even older character I thought up as a kid when watching Martin Mystery on Teletoon. An alien queen I named Rena. I fleshed out her design to make her more fish like while I made her attire a bit reminiscent of Wonderwoman (movie).
